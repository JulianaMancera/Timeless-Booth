How It Works

01

Enable the camera

Grant camera access when your browser asks. Nothing is recorded until you press the shutter.

02

Strike four poses

A 3‑2‑1 countdown and a flash fire for each of four shots, just like a real photobooth.

03

Download your strip

Your four shots develop into a sepia-toned strip you can save as a PNG.
